Our Location

Find Us on the Map

We are located in Kimana town along the Loitokitok Road — easily accessible from Nairobi (approx. 3.5 hrs) and minutes from the Amboseli–Kimana Gate.

🗺️
Vintex Guest House, Kimana
Approx. coordinates: 2°41′S, 37°31′E · Kajiado County, Kenya
Open in Google Maps ↗
🚌
From Nairobi

Take the Loitokitok bus from Machakos Country Bus Station. Approx. 3.5 hrs. Alight at Kimana stage.

🚗
Self Drive

Take Mombasa Road to Emali, then turn off towards Loitokitok. Follow signs to Kimana. Road is tarmac.

🏕️
From Amboseli

Exit via the Kimana Gate. We are 2 km from the gate on the main Kimana–Loitokitok road.
