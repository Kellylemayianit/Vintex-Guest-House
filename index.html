Our Story

A Home at the Edge of the Wild

Vintex Guest House was born from a simple belief — that travellers deserve a calm, clean, and genuinely welcoming place to rest after a day of adventure. Located in Kimana town, we are perfectly positioned as your base for Amboseli National Park, Chyulu Hills, and the greater Tsavo corridor.

Every room is thoughtfully furnished, every meal made with local ingredients, and every guest treated like family. We pride ourselves on personal service, honest value, and the kind of warmth that keeps guests coming back.
